# fmcg-nrm-analysis-excel
## Net Revenue Management (NRM) Analysis – FMCG Shampoo Category (Excel Case Study)

### Overview
This project analyzes sales performance and identifies growth opportunities for a fictional FMCG company, HealthMax, using Excel. It applies Net Revenue Management (NRM) principles to drive revenue growth and improve profitability through data-driven decisions.

### Objectives
- Analyze market performance using key KPIs (market share, growth)
- Build time-based metrics (YTD, MAT)
- Identify high-impact NRM opportunities across pricing, product mix, promotions, and price-pack architecture
- Forecast baseline sales and quantify incremental impact of NRM initiatives

### Dataset
- External sales data (Jan 2018– Mar 2023): units and value by brand, region, and category
- Promotion data: baseline sales, uplift, ROI
- Internal product data: price, COGS, volume
- New product assumptions for launch analysis

### Key Analyses

#### Performance Analysis
- Calculated market share and growth (YoY, YTD, MAT)
- Identified top-performing brands and regions

#### Time-Based Metrics
- Built YTD and MAT calculations using Excel formulas

#### Mix Management
- Analyzed product-level net sales and profitability
- Identified high-revenue and high-margin products

#### Market Expansion
- Identified organic shampoo as fastest-growing segment
- Estimated 2024 market size (~1.02M units)

#### New Product Launch
- Evaluated product options based on net sales and profitability
- Recommended Herbashine for higher gross profit

#### Price-Pack Architecture
- Proposed 50ml travel-size variant
- Estimated ~$266K incremental net sales

#### Promotion Analysis
- Calculated ROI of promotions
- Identified most effective promotion (12% ROI)

#### Forecasting & Impact
- Forecasted 2024 baseline sales (~$20.8M)
- Quantified incremental impact using waterfall analysis

### Results
- Baseline 2024 forecast: $20.8M  
- Final projected sales with NRM: $21.4M  
- NRM initiatives contributed more growth than natural market trends

### Tools
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, conditional aggregation functions, forecasting, and data visualization)
